About

I’m a Northwestern alum and Venture for America fellow living and working in Columbus, Ohio. When I’m not at Print Syndicate, I spend my time playing tennis, reading, watching movies, listening to music and podcasts, and learning web development. New York is home.

I’m interested in startups, social entrepreneurship, food and health, technology, and media. Right now, I’m hoping to learn more about companies that are focused on positive social impact as an aspect of overall performance, as well as computer programming and the role of technology in social enterprise and journalism. If you’d like to chat, please reach out.
